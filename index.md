### Step 2: Check Your System Requirements

Before you proceed, please ensure that your system meets the following requirements:

- **Supported Systems:** UEFI compatible motherboard
- **Minimum Storage:** 10 MB free space
- **Recommended RAM:** 1 GB or more

### Step 3: Installation Instructions

1. After downloading the application from the Releases page, locate the downloaded file.
2. Copy the `SnakePkg.efi` file to a USB drive formatted with FAT32.
3. Make sure your USB drive is easily accessible. You may want to label the drive for easy identification.

### Step 4: Boot into UEFI

1. Restart your computer.
2. Access your UEFI/BIOS settings. This usually involves pressing a key (like F2, DEL, or ESC) during boot. Please refer to your computer's manual or screen prompts for the specific key.
3. In the UEFI settings, locate the "Boot" section.
4. Ensure that the USB drive is set as a primary boot device.

### Step 5: Launch the Game

1. Save your UEFI settings and exit. Your computer will reboot.
2. You should see the Snake game interface appear on the screen.
3. Use your keyboard keys (usually arrow keys) to control the snake and start playing.
